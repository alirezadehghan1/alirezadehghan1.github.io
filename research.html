Research

Here are the abstracts of my research experiences during my undergraduate studies.

XAI Approach to Predict Burnout and Depression among Medical Students

This research explores the application of explainable AI to predict burnout and depression in medical students. It focuses on improving mental health awareness using interpretable models.

Knowledge Discovery for Anthropometric Measures Using Data Mining Techniques

In this study, we utilized data mining techniques to uncover patterns in anthropometric measures, providing insights into ergonomic design and healthcare applications.

Time Series Forecasting of Active Customers Using Sequence Models: A Comparative Evaluation

This paper presents a comparative evaluation of sequence models for forecasting active customer trends in a retail context, highlighting model effectiveness and practical implications.

Assessing the Potential of Machine Learning in Medical Diagnosis

This project investigates machine learning's potential in enhancing diagnostic accuracy, particularly in healthcare applications, through predictive modeling.
